The World With NCT

NCT is an abbreviation of 'Neo Culture Technology', and as it was created as a new cultural technology, it is a completely new concept group with 'openness' and 'extensibility' as the main points, free recruitment of members, and no limit on the number of members. Under the brand NCT, each team based in a city around the world will debut sequentially, and plans to continuously showcase various combinations and transformations of members through 'NCT U'.



NCT DREAM

(CHENLE, JENO, JAEMIN, RENJUN, MARK, HAECHAN & JISUNG)

NCT Dream (Korean: 엔시티 드림) is the third sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT, formed by SM Entertainment in 2016. They were initially intended to be the teenaged unit of NCT with an admission-and-graduation system, in which members would leave after reaching age of majority (20 in Korean age reckoning, 19 internationally), prior to re-branding in 2020.

NCT 127

(HAECHAN, TAEIL, JOHNNY, TAEYONG, YUTA, DOYOUNG, JAEHYUN, JUNGWOO, MARK)

NCT 127 (Korean: 엔시티 127; RR: ensiti il-i-chil) is the first fixed and second overall sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT, formed and managed by SM Entertainment. The current lineup consists of nine active members: Taeil, Johnny, Taeyong, Yuta, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Jungwoo, Mark and Haechan.[3] NCT 127 are known for their experimental hip hop and R&B-based music and energetic choreographies.

WAYV

(KUN, TEN, WINWIN, LUCAS, XIAOJUN, HENDERY & YANGYANG)

WayV (Chinese: 威神V; pinyin: WēiShén V; an initialism for We are your Vision)[5] is a Chinese boy band and the fourth overall sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT,[6][7] managed by SM Entertainment's Chinese sub-label, Label V. The group is composed of seven members: Kun, Ten, Winwin, Lucas, Xiaojun, Hendery, and Yangyang. They debuted on January 17, 2019, with the digital extended play (EP) The Vision.
